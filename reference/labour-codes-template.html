Code on Wages, 2019
Act No. 29 of 2019 · Assented 8 Aug 2019
4
Acts Replaced
Payment of Wages Act, 1936
88 years old
→
What it covered: Timely payment of wages, deductions, wage periods.
Key change: Now applies to all employees. Digital payment enabled. Wage period max 1 month.
Minimum Wages Act, 1948
78 years old
→
What it covered: Scheduled employment minimum wages.
Key change: Floor wage concept. Universal coverage. Basic+DA ≥50% of total remuneration.
Payment of Bonus Act, 1965
61 years old
→
What it covered: Statutory bonus for establishments with 20+.
Key change: Calculation basis aligned with new wage definition. 8.33% min to 20% max.
Equal Remuneration Act, 1976
50 years old
→
What it covered: Equal pay for equal work regardless of gender.
Key change: Prohibition on gender discrimination in recruitment.
Key Provisions
Basic + DA ≥ 50% of total remuneration
Unified wage definition across all codes
Floor wage by Central Government
Payment within 7 days (monthly)
Overtime at 2× ordinary rate
Industrial Relations Code, 2020
Act No. 35 of 2020 · Assented 28 Sep 2020
3
Acts Replaced
Trade Unions Act, 1926
100 years old
→
What it covered: Registration and regulation of trade unions.
Key change: Negotiating union concept. 51% membership for sole negotiating agent.
Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1946
80 years old
→
What it covered: Terms of employment in industrial establishments.
Key change: Threshold raised to 300 workers. Model standing orders apply by default.
Industrial Disputes Act, 1947
79 years old
→
What it covered: Investigation and settlement of industrial disputes.
Key change: Retrenchment threshold raised to 300 (from 100). 14-day strike notice. Re-skilling fund.
Key Provisions
Standing orders threshold: 300 workers
Retrenchment approval: 300 workers
Fixed-term employment with parity
FTE gratuity after 1 year
14-day strike/lockout notice
Code on Social Security, 2020
Act No. 36 of 2020 · Assented 28 Sep 2020
9
Acts Replaced
Employees' Provident Funds Act, 1952
74 years old
→
What it covered: PF, pension, insurance for employees.
Key change: 20+ employee threshold. Wage definition aligned. Aadhaar-linked portability.
Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948
78 years old
→
What it covered: Health insurance for industrial workers.
Key change: ESIC extended PAN-India. Coverage for gig workers possible.
Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972
54 years old
→
What it covered: Gratuity on retirement, resignation, death.
Key change: 10+ employee threshold. FTE eligible after 1 year. Calculation per 26 days.
Maternity Benefit Act, 1961
65 years old
→
What it covered: Maternity leave and benefits.
Key change: 26 weeks for first two children. WFH option. Crèche requirement.
Plus 5 other Acts
Various
→
Employees' Compensation Act 1923, Building Workers Acts (2) 1996, Cine Workers Welfare Fund Act 1981, Unorganised Workers' Social Security Act 2008.
Key Provisions
Gig & platform workers included
Aadhaar-linked portability
ESIC extended PAN-India
FTE gratuity after 1 year
Aggregator contribution model
OSH & Working Conditions Code, 2020
Act No. 37 of 2020 · Assented 28 Sep 2020
13
Acts Replaced
Factories Act, 1948
78 years old
→
What it covered: Health, safety, welfare in factories.
Key change: Women can work night shifts with safeguards. Working hours flexibility.
Contract Labour Act, 1970
56 years old
→
What it covered: Regulation of contract labour.
Key change: Core activity restrictions. Principal employer liability enhanced.
Inter-State Migrant Workmen Act, 1979
47 years old
→
What it covered: Protection of interstate migrant workers.
Key change: Registration requirement. Journey allowance. Helpline.
Plus 10 other sector-specific Acts
Various
→
Mines Act, Plantation Labour Act, Dock Workers Act, Motor Transport Workers Act, Newspaper Workers Act, Working Journalists Act, Sales Promotion Employees Act, Beedi Workers Acts (2), Shops & Establishments (state).
Key Provisions
Single registration/licence
Mandatory appointment letters
Contract labour core activity restriction
Women night shift with safeguards
Leave: 1 day per 20 days worked
